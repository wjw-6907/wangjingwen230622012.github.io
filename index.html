3. 法庭意见书要素 (English)

Definition: The formal title identifying the parties and court.

Example: A.L.A. Schechter Poultry Corporation v. United States

案件标题是法律文件的官方名称，明确诉讼双方及管辖法院。

Citation: 295 U.S. 495 (1935)

这是查找本案的标准引用格式，包括卷号、报告系列和页码。

The Schechter brothers were convicted under the National Industrial Recovery Act (NIRA) for violating the Live Poultry Code in New York.

谢克特兄弟因违反国家工业复兴法（NIRA）下的活禽准则而在纽约被定罪。

Issue: Does Congress exceed its commerce power by regulating intrastate poultry sales, and is the delegation of legislative authority under NIRA unconstitutional?

国会通过规制州内家禽销售是否超出其商业权？NIRA下的立法权委托是否违宪？

Holding: The Court unanimously held the NIRA unconstitutional on both commerce clause and non-delegation grounds.

法院一致裁定NIRA在商业条款和不可委托原则上均违宪。

Chief Justice Hughes distinguished direct and indirect effects on interstate commerce and found no adequate standards for delegation.

首席大法官休斯区分了对州际商业的直接与间接影响，并认定委托缺乏充分标准。
